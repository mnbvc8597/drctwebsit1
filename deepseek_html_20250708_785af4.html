About Us

Established in 1992, DRCT is a non-profit organization dedicated to empowering rural women through education and skills training. Since 2014-15, DRCT has been actively involved in government-sponsored skill development programs including:

  • STAR
  • NDLM
  • PMKVY
  • AICTE
  • TNSDC
  • NULM
  • DDUGKY

Recognized as a Skill Hub, DRCT provides industry-relevant training to underprivileged youth to enhance their employability and entrepreneurial potential. DRCT has demonstrated a proven track record under DDU-GKY, achieving a 75% placement rate, significantly contributing to the socio-economic upliftment of rural communities.
